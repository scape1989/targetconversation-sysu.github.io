Overview

What is Target-oriented Conversation?

    Target-oriented dialogue is a branch of the dialogue system. The whole round of dialogue is for a certain goal, such as medical consultation, or a topic to reach, etc.


Dataset

 Details on downloading the latest dataset are available on the dataset download webpage.


 For medical dialogue, we have derived two datasets:

  • ⊕

    [NEW] CovidDialog-CH: Chinese Medical Dialogue Dataset for COVID19 Consultant

  • 12789 consultations, each consultation has at least 8 senteces
  • ⊕

    DX Medical Dialogue Dataset built from a Chinese online health-care community

  • 5 types of diseases, 41 symptoms
  • 527 conversational data in total. 423 conversational data the training set, 104 for testing


 For target-guided open-domain conversation, we have derived two keyword-augmented conversation datasets:

  • ⊕

    An English TGODC dataset from the persona-chat corpus

  • 8,939 train conversations, 500 val conversations, 500 test conversations
  • 101,935 train utterances, 5,602 val utterances, 5,317 test utterances
  • ⊕

    A Chinese TGODC dataset from the Weibo corpus

  • 824,742 train conversations, 45,824 val conversations, 45,763 test conversations
  • 1,104,438 train utterances, 60,893 val utterances, 60,893 test utterances
