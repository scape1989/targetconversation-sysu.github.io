Overview

What is Target-oriented Conversation?

 Many practical open-domain dialogue applications have specific goals to achieve, e.g., psychotherapy, education, recommendation.
 To bridge the gap between open-domain chit-chat and task-oriented dialogue, we propose a new task called ** target-guided open-domain conversation **. Given a target and a starting utterance of the conversation, an agent is asked to chat with a user starting from an arbitrary topic, and proactively guide the conversation to the target.


Dataset

 Details on downloading the latest dataset may be found on the dataset download webpage.
 We have derived two keyword-augmented conversation datasets for the study:

  • ⊕

    An English TGODC dataset from the persona-chat corpus

  • 8,939 train conversations, 500 val conversations, 500 test conversations
  • 101,935 train utterances, 5,602 val utterances, 5,317 test utterances
  • ⊕

    A Chinese TGODC dataset from the Weibo corpus

  • 824,742 train conversations, 45,824 val conversations, 45,763 test conversations
  • 1,104,438 train utterances, 60,893 val utterances, 60,893 test utterances
